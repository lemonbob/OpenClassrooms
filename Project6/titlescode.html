SETTINGS

In 2149, after the cyborgs and humans had almost obliterated each other, an uneasy truce was struck. Under this truce, a contest was formed where humans and cyborgs would battle ... to the death. This contest was know as "Cyborg Arena".

In the Cyborg Arena, there are two phases to combat. First, players must negotiate the arena to collect the most power weapon they can. However, dark tiles show the path of randomly generated energy beams from the beam generators. Avoid being caught under these beams, they will cause instant death.

Weapons are swapped by landing on, or passing over, weapon squares. Be careful, weapons can be swapped more than once when more than one weapon square is crossed in a move. Blue squares show all valid moves.

In the second phase, when a player lands on, or passes by, a square adjacent to another player, combat commences. A special blue square shows when a move will result in combat.

When combat starts, it is a battle to the death, there is no escape. During combat, players may attack or defend the next shot. Many weapons do variable amounts of damage. Defending a large hit could be better than chosing to attack!

May fortune favour the brave...
